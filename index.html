What does this tool do?

This tool finds the optimal hoard upgrade strategy for reaching hoard quality 10 and level 100 using as few resources as possible. Enter the amount of each treasure troop you have along with your current hoard stats to generate the optimal troop combinations for each upgrade step.

Why quality 10 and level 100?

Hoard quality 10 gives you the highest treasure chest upgrades each time you run the delve, maximizing your rewards. Hoard level 100 is the highest one that counts toward faction renown. Higher levels increase your troop stats in the delve further but are generally considered a late-game investment mostly for beating high level delves with all-faction teams.

How do you handle the hoard quality upgrade chance?

Most of the troop combinations shown will upgrade the quality 100% of the time. For troop combinations that don't give a 100% chance of upgrading the quality, no upgrade is assumed.

Does it always find the best possible solution?

The algorithm is pretty brute force and cuts a few corners to stay fast. So you may be able to find a slightly better solution in rare cases.
