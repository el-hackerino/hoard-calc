What does this do?

This tool finds the optimal hoard upgrade strategy for reaching hoard quality 10 and level 100 using as few resources as possible. Enter the amount of each treasure troop you have along with your current hoard stats to generate the optimal troop combinations for each upgrade step.

Why quality 10 and level 100?

Hoard quality 10 gives you the highest treasure chest upgrades each time you run the delve, maximizing your rewards. Hoard level 100 is the highest that counts toward faction renown. Higher levels increase your troop stats in the delve further but are not generally considered a worthwhile investment.

Is the generated solution optimal?

The algorithm is pretty brute force and cuts a few corners. So you may be able to find a slightly better solution sometimes, but this should be pretty rare.
